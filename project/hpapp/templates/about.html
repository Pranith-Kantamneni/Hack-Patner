What is Hack-Partner?

A platform for hackathon enthusiasts to find their perfect team

Find Team Members

Looking for a developer, designer, or project manager for your hackathon team? Post your requirements and connect with talented individuals.

Join Hackathons

Showcase your skills and find teams looking for your expertise. Browse hackathons and join the ones that match your interests.

Anonymous Chats

Connect with potential team members through our anonymous chat feature. Discuss ideas, skills, and expectations before revealing your identity.

Privacy First

Your privacy is our priority. Choose when and with whom to share your personal information.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email and create an anonymous profile with your skills and interests.

2

Browse or Post

Browse available hackathons and team requests or post your own team requirements.

3

Connect & Chat

Connect with potential team members through our anonymous chat feature.

4

Once you've found your perfect match, form your team and get ready to hack!
